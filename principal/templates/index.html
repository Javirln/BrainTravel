What is this?

BrainTravel is the perfect tool for all travellers. It automatically creates a trip planning for you, offering a daily schedule with the highest rated sites in the area. It also has an internal network in which users can interact between them regarding trips they made, commenting and sharing experiences.

Features

Automatic plans

Day by day plan for your trip with a few clicks based on others travellers recommendations.

Constraints

Give us restrictions for your trip and we'll do the rest for you.

Satistics

Travel stats for your own orientation.

Social trips

Share experiences with other users.
